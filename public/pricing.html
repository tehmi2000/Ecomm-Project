Univers Pricing

An overview of the internal pricing structure of Univers and its affiliates

How Expensive Are We?

Often times, we want to know how expensive the items in a store or an online shopping platform like Jumia, Konga, or eBay are but you cant find anything online about price comparison. Due to this reason, Univers has given its customers to right spot to get a heads-up about its prices. Here is a comprehensive list of how Univers manages its prices and subscription plans for its users

1. Store

A store on univers is like your online shop where you can stock your items. By default, every vendor has a basic store and is allowed to post as much as the store can take. Consider upgrading to a better package for a better user experience and flexibility.

Upgrade Your Store

Basic Pack
The Basic Pack is the default plan for every registered vendor on the platform. With the Basic Pack, you have the ability to trade on the platform comfortably.
Pro Pack
The Pro Pack is a compromise between the Basic Pack and the Mega Pack as you have the ability to trade comfortably and you can get updates on public reactions to your product.
Upgrade for $5
Mega Pack
The Mega Pack is the biggest pack of all which gives you total acces to all features available to vendors, and with zero limitation on the size of your store and what can be sold.
Upgrade for $25
