Frequently Asked Questions

1. What does Univers do? Univers is a shopping platform for anyone that wants to buy and sell their items
2. How can I start selling my items on the platform? To post an item for sale, you have to register for vendorship after registering as a user on the platform. Visit our sign up page to register as a user on the platform.
3. How does Univers make its money? Univers makes its money majorly from VAT charged on product when a trade is successfully completed. You can check out the pricing page to learn more
4. Who are vendors? Vendors are registered sellers/traders/store owners on the platform. Visit our How to become a vendor page to learn more
5. Can I hide my store items so that nobody sees them? Yes, you can. To post or hide an item in your store from the public, click on the toggle button at the bottom right of the item and it would either be toggled off or on. That way you can choose the items you wish to display to the public.
